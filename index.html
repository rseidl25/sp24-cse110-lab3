3. Offensive Game Plan

The following players are included in the offensive scheme we will be discussing today:

Starting Offense Roster
  • QB: Dak Prescott
  • RB: Tony Pollard
  • WR1: CeeDee Lamb
  • WR2: Brandin Cooks
  • WR3: Michael Gallup
  • TE: Jake Ferguson
  • C: Brock Hoffman
  • G: Zack Martin
  • G: T.J. Bass
  • OT: Tyler Smith
  • OT: Terence Steele

To win the game on offense, we must accomplish the following:

  1. Protect the football.

    We cannot have any turnovers on offense whether it be a fumble or an interception.

  2. Don't play conservatively.

    We have to come out firing on offense. To win this game, we must take an early lead and hold onto it for the rest of the game.

  3. Feed CeeDee Lamb.

    CeeDee has been too dominant this season to not give him the ball in this game. We should rely on him as a downfield threat.

  4. Get the run game going early.

    Pollard and Dowdle must step up in the run game. We must be a threat on the ground and in the air if we want to win.


4. Defensive Game Plan

The following players are included in the defensive scheme we will be discussing today:

Starting Defense Roster
  • LDE: DeMarcus Lawrence
  • LDT: Chauncey Golston
  • RDT: Osa Odighizuwa
  • RDE: Micah Parsons
  • WLB: Damone Clark
  • MLB: Markquese Bell
  • SLB: DeMarvion Overshown
  • LCB: DaRon Bland
  • SS: Donovan Wilson
  • FF: Malik Hooker
  • RCB: C.J. Goodwin

To win the game on defense, we must accomplish the following:

  1. Force turnovers.

    We need to hold the ball as much as possible in this game. That means punching the ball out of the runner's hands and jumping routes for picks.

  2. Stop Aaron Jones.

    Everyone knows how much of a threat Jones is once he gets going. We need to shut down Jones and the run game immediately.

  3. Pressure the QB.

    Jordan Love is only a true threat if he's comfortable in the pocket. Our D line and edge rushers must put as much pressure on Love as possible.

  4. Stay disciplined on the line.

    We cannot give up yardage through penalties. Watch the ball and stay disciplined.


5. Special Teams Game Plan

The following players are included in the special teams scheme we will be discussing today:

Starting Special Teams Roster
  • PK: Brandon Aubrey
  • P: Bryan Anger
  • H: Bryan Anger
  • PR: KaVontae Turpin
  • KR: KaVontae Turpin
  • LS: Trent Sieg

To win the game on special teams, we must accomplish the following:

  1. Protect the kicker.

    If we allow any of our kicks or punts to be blocked, we will lose the game. Plain and simple.

  2. Trick plays.

    Depending on the situation, we may elect to fake a punt on 4th down. Be ready for this call in the game.

  3. Kick return TDs.

    During kick returns, continue blocking your man until you hear a whistle. Special teams TDs will win us the game.
